About Caravela Beach Resort

Caravela Beach Resort is a luxurious **5-star property** nestled along the pristine shores of **Varca Beach in South Goa**. Spanning **24 acres** of lush tropical gardens, the resort offers elegant accommodations, multiple dining options, a spa, and recreational facilities. Known for its Portuguese-inspired architecture and warm hospitality, it provides a perfect blend of comfort and beachfront serenity. The architecture of the hotel reflects colonial charm, with wooden interiors and vintage decor creating a warm and welcoming ambiance.
